Opportunity

  • +/- 25 rigs to schedule around +/- 750 well candidates
  • schedule prioritization is based on tiering system from estimated oil recovery (single variable)
  • limited human capability to define most optimum schedule. Difficult to revise the schedule.

Innovation

  • Developed AI-based technique to optimize WOWS execution schedule prioritization by incorporating several variables: real distance between wells, LPO, other economic values such as NPV/DPI, compliance.
  • Genetic Algorithm (GA) is very popular heuristic approach for defining optimum solution with multi objective searching capability.

Business Results

  • Deployed in under 4 months
  • value creation of $0.3 MM for 4 months implementation
  • Robust schedule
