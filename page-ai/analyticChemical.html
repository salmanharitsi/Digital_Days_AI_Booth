Opportunity

  • Chemical stimulation can clean up the wellbore (eliminate the plugging due to scale and organic deposition) in order to increase the oil production
  • Duri field consist of 6000 producer wells. Historically chemical stimulation was executed with rate 100 jobs/month, but it is expected 300 jobs/month now.
  • Difficult to classify well because there are so many aspect which need to be analyzed and it does not have standard procedure. Success rate of chemical stimulation job ~55%

Innovation

  • Developed AI-based technique to make chemical stimulation candidate
  • Developed two machine learning model to predict the oil gain after job and to classify the chemical stimulation job types (acidizing or solvent)
  • Automatic chemical stimulation job candidacy empower increased number of job execution

Business Results

  • Deployed in under 3 months
  • Potential value creation Rp 100-165 M (target success rate 70%)
