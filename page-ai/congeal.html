Solution

Develop tool for congeal prediction and prevention by implementing hybrid solution: technical analytics (fundamental) and data-science

If day 8 is predicted high pressure by hybrid method on segment 7 then inject PPD at Menggala North GS

Outcome

During rainy season (Nov 1st, 2018 to Now) no congeal event was recorded using prediction and prevention tool

Situation

  • Oil is naturally so viscous thus may cause many flow assurance problems
  • WK Rokan has experienced several oil congealing events during rainy seasons
  • Pipelines were congealed causing production loss and pipe rupture and leakage
  • Many factors may aggravate oil congealing e.g. aging pipeline, long length of pipeline, lack of external insulation, soil interaction, low fluid velocity, other deposits and high rainfall intensity

Oppoturnity

  • There many data available to learn congeal phenomenon based on historical data.
  • Weather forecast can be retrieved from service provider to help rain prediction
  • There is correlation between temperature and pipeline temperature that describe the wax formation
