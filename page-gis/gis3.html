Background

In accordance with the Company direction to strengthen the HSSE procedures, we required to maintain the safety of all workers and assets thus the productivity is not disrupted. Several programs have been conducted by the AIRE HES Committee in collaboration with the PIC Contract Owner to complete an obligation for contractors to do V&V coach to the field crew. Field workers are also required to perform tailgate meeting at the work site before conducting field work activities. Integrated systems are needed to ensure and monitor both activities.

User Requirement

  • An integrated system to monitor :
  • Tailgate Meeting has been conducted, including information of workers numbers and vehicles used.
  • V&V Visit has been performed, to fulfill site visit compliance of monitoring crew in the field.
  • A system that can be operated by any perwira and mitra kerja related to this job;
  • A mobile application that can input and store data in conditions of no signal;

Solution

  • Mobile application forms using ArcGIS Survey123
  • Tailgate Meeting Monitoring
  • V&V Visit Monitoring
  • Reporting Dashboards using ArcGIS Dashboard:
  • Tailgate Meeting Monitoring Dashboard
  • V&V Visit Monitoring Dashboard

Mobile App - V&V Visit Monitoring Form
