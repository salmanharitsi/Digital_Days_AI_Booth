Background

Pertamina Hulu Rokan (PHR) operates Minas injection field which consist of three (3) main areas, named Block-1, Block-2, and Block-3. In line with PHR plans to increase the production capacity of the Minas Field, an adequacy check of the existing facility is required in order to discover bottlenecks and identify the required optimizations needed in the system. An accurate digital representation is therefore needed to achieve such objectives swiftly and efficiently.

User Requirement

  • providing a high-fidelity Minas Injection Network simulator, powered by the PIPESIM technology, which will act as a digital twin of the Minas field. Through the use of digital twin, suboptimal planning and decision making can be eliminated and faster identification of production optimization opportunities can be made possible.
  • Connect to Datamart, Realtime sensor data, ORDC data for AI simulation process in Dataiku.
  • To establish auto-calibration and smart advisory system to automatically update the model to the latest field condition via connection to injection database, and provide weekly required insights through network simulation and optimizer runs.
